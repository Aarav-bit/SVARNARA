Privacy Policy

SVARNARA Fine Dining collects reservation and contact details only to respond to guest enquiries, manage table requests, and improve hospitality service.

Form submissions may include name, email, phone number, visit date, party size, and special requests. Do not submit sensitive medical or payment information through website forms.

For this internship demo, submissions are stored locally in the browser unless a secure form endpoint is configured by the site owner.
